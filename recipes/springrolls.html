Spring Rolls!

Meat in veggies wrapped in rice paper!

Enjoy this vietnamese dish!

Ingredients

  • For the roll:
  • Rice paper (dried)
  • Fresh Thai Basil
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Lettuce
  • Cooked meat of your choice (chicken, pork, beef, shrimp)
  • Any additonal veggies you prefer

  • For the sauce:
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1/4 cup of Water
  • 4 Tspn Fish Sauce
  • 3 Tbsp Hoison sauce
  • 1 Fresh Limes
  • 1 Tbsp Garlic Chili Sauce (optional - for heat)
  • 1 Tbsp Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. First make the sauce by mixing all ingridents together. Adjust hoison/peanut butter and water for a silky smooth sauce
  2. In a large bowl of warm water, dip one wrapper in for a few seconds to soak the dry rice paper
  3. Lay rice paper on flat surface
  4. First place lettuce in the center, then add meats, then any additional veggies you prefer, top with cilantro/mint/basil
  5. Carefully roll the spring roll by rolling inwards from the bottom of the spring roll, and tightly packing it as you roll up
  6. Seal the ends and you should be done!
