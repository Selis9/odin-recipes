Pho Soup!

A nice savory vietnamese noodle soup!

Enjoy this vietnamese soup everyone has tried before! Salty, savory, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon Sticks
  • Onion
  • Star Anise
  • Coriander
  • Ginger
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Fish Sauce
  • Beef or Pork Bones

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in large pot
  2. Parboil bones for 5 minutes for the initial impurities to seep out
  3. Drain pot and rinse bones to make sure all impurities are removed
  4. Bring new pot of water to a boil
  5. While waiting for water to boil, toast first 5 aromatic Ingredients to bring out aromatic flavors, slightly charring onion and ginger
  6. Carefully throw bones back in, along with toasted aromatics and lower to a simmer
  7. Simmer for at minimum 2 hours for best flavor
  8. Adjust with Salt, Sugar, and Fish sauce as needed - for saltiness, sweetness, and umami
  9. When ready, serve with fresh cooked noodles, veggies, and meats of your choice
